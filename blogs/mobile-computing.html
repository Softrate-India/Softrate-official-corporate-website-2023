Mobile Computing

Mobile communication is a human-computer interaction in which a computer is expected to transport during normal usage. Mobile computing played a significant role in the fields like business and in public sector organizations. There are six major types of mobile computing devices which includes laptop, computers, notebook computers and these three are called portable computers. Personal digital assistants(PDAs), smartphones and portable data terminals are called hand-held computers. Mobile computing involves mobile communication, mobile hardware and mobile software.

Features of Mobile Computing

  • Fast processor speed.
  • Easy to handle the small devices.
  • Data can be transferred easily.
  • Huge memory capacity.
  • Good battery life. Global Positioning System.
  • Durability.
  • It is easy to connect to other computers and transmit data.

    • Mobile communication includes ad hoc networks, infrastructure networks and it also includes the communication properties, data formats and concrete technologies. Mobile hardware includes mobile devices or device components.

    Business: :

    With the help of a computing system, he or she can represent their ideas like a presentation in front of their clients. If it is in online mode, through video conferencing we can discuss with the team about the projects going on. Suppose, if the travelling salesman wants to access the company’s database, he can retrieve the data on a wireless device and it helps to maintain the consistency.

    Traffic: :

    When travelling in heavy traffic, if we want to know about the latest news or want to listen to music, we can easily broadcast the data through DAB which is digital audio broadcasting. If we are unaware of the desired location, we use a global positioning system(GPS).

    Emergency situations:

    Mobile computing plays a significant role in the medical sector. The wireless connection can carry the information from the injured persons and can consult the particular doctor to recover. In worst conditions like earthquakes, tsunami, flood, fire communication plays a vital role with mobile computing. It handles the situation easily.

    Replacement of Fixed network:

    Wired network has been replaced with wireless network. In wired networks like weather forecasting, earthquake detection, it is impossible to get the environmental data. It is possible when you adapt the fixed network in computing.

    Infotainment:

    Wireless networks are capable of delivering the latest information at any suitable region. Wireless network application lies in entertainment and games for example ad-hoc gaming networks. Infotainment by wireless computing is easy when we compare it with other computing.
